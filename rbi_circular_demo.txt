RESERVE BANK OF INDIA
CIRCULAR

Ref. No. RBI/2026-045/INFOSEC
Date: May 17, 2026

To,
All Regulated Entities (REs)

Subject: Master Direction on Data Protection and Cyber Security

1. Introduction
The Reserve Bank of India, in the interest of securing customer data, issues this Master Direction. All Regulated Entities (REs) must comply with the following mandates immediately.

2. Mandates
2.1 All REs shall implement AES-256 encryption for all customer Personally Identifiable Information (PII) at rest.
2.2 REs must enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all privileged administrative accounts accessing core banking systems.
2.3 It is mandatory that all external-facing APIs utilize TLS 1.3 or higher.

3. Reporting
3.1 REs shall maintain immutable audit logs of all critical actions and ensure a quarterly third-party review is conducted.
3.2 Any cyber security incident must be reported to the central RBI cyber cell within 6 hours of detection.

Compliance with these directions is mandatory. Non-compliance will attract penal action under Section 47-A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949.
